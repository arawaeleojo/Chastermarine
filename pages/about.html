ABOUT CHASTAR MARINE & ENGINEERING SERVICES LTD

Our Core Principles

Our Mission

To deliver innovative and reliable marine and engineering solutions that exceed our clients' expectations.

Our Vision

To become leaders in modern technology services and products, earning good returns and growing our investments.

Core Values

Our clients' satisfaction is our priority, driving every aspect of our project execution and service delivery.

Decades of Marine & Engineering Experience

Our team has acquired a wide variety of experience in technical execution and service delivery procedures, meeting clients' complex expectations across the marine, engineering, and lifting operation sectors.

Since being incorporated on October 25th, 2006, we have been involved in prime EPC-projects, consistently providing critical services and engineering/design support for specific contract scopes. Our history is built on successful execution.

Expertise, Discipline and Precision

CHASTAR MARINE & ENGINEERING SERVICES LTD is wholly an indigenous Marine, Engineering, Procurement, and Lifting services organization with 100% Nigerian shareholding at its hierarchy.

The organization is managed by a board comprising seasoned professionals and technocrats with the ultimate drive to promote indigenous technical participation in Nigeria's infrastructure development service chain. We are committed to local content excellence.
