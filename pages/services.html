Our Core Expertise (Original Services)

Marine Maintenance, Repairs & Dry-docking

We provide full vessel care, from routine servicing to complex repairs and dry-docking, ensuring safety, reliability, and compliance at sea.

Fabrication & Welding

Our state-of-the-art fabrication and welding services meet the highest industry standards for structural integrity and quality.
